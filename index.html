CeliacScanner

iOS App for Gluten-Free Living

A personal project born from a real need, as someone with celiac disease who loves to travel, I often struggle to read ingredient labels in foreign languages. CeliacScanner uses OCR technology to instantly scan and analyze food labels, detecting potential gluten-containing ingredients.

  • Dual-mode scanning: barcode lookup and ingredient text recognition
  • Multilingual OCR support (Greek, English, and 100+ languages)
  • Instant safety assessment with clear visual indicators
  • Offline mode for travel without internet
  • Scan history with SwiftData persistence
